Traffic APIs

Traffic APIs seamlessly integrate real-time and historical traffic data, helping your users stay ahead of congestion. Developers can utilize these APIs to enhance applications, boost transportation efficiency, and save time and fuel. Whether for drivers, fleets, on-demand services (like ride-hailing and food delivery), or traffic management authorities, our solutions reduce stress and improve overall traffic management

Updated Every 10 Seconds

Comprehensive Road Network Coverage

Traffic Prediction

We combine actual and forecasted vehicle speeds for each upcoming road segment, enabling intelligent route planning.

We calculate speeds at the lane level, providing detailed traffic information and more precise estimated times of arrival (ETAs).

Early Safety Warnings

Traffic Insights and Analysis

Our traffic insights enable road authorities and traffic management centers to effectively reduce congestion and lower emissions.
