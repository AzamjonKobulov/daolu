Places APIs

Our suite of Places API products transforms the way businesses and developers utilize location data. Whether you're delivering fast, relevant search results with the Search API, optimizing data processing through the Batch Search API, or translating geographic coordinates with the Geocoding API, our tools empower your operations to perform at their best.

  • Get started

Precision Search

Leverage the Search API to deliver highly accurate and relevant results, enhancing location-based queries to create optimal user experiences and facilitate the seamless discovery of Points of Interest (POIs).

Efficient Large-Scale Data Processing

Optimize data handling with the Batch Search API, designed to process multiple queries efficiently in a single request, perfect for large-scale operations.

Geocoding and Reverse Geocoding

Harness the capabilities of our Geocoding APIs to convert addresses and places into precise geographic coordinates, or vice versa, enabling powerful location-based functionality.

Effortless Integration of Location Services

Easily integrate APIs into your applications, whether you're enhancing search functionality, managing large datasets, or incorporating geocoding features to meet your specific needs.

Real-Time EV Charging Information

Provide users with up-to-the-minute information on electric vehicle (EV) charging stations through the EV Charging Stations Availability API, simplifying the planning of efficient and eco-friendly journeys.

Autocomplete for Smarter Searches

Enhance user experience with autocomplete, quickly recognizing entities within input queries and delivering the most relevant and accurate search results in less time.

Precise Address Validation

Accurately identify address numbers, street names, postal codes, and Points of Interest (POIs) with our advanced address validation features, supported by precise location data.

Comprehensive Geospatial Insights

Utilize geospatial data across our API suite to drive informed decision-making and enhance business intelligence, improving both your applications and overall operations.

Search API
